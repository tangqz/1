## 2025-01-26 - [Localized ARIA Labels]
**Learning:** When adding accessibility labels to an app with a specific language (e.g., Chinese), ensure the `aria-label` attributes match the UI language (e.g., "最小化" instead of "Minimize") to provide a consistent experience for screen reader users.
**Action:** Always check `date-fns` locale or existing text to determine the correct language for accessibility attributes.
